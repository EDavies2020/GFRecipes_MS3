ABOUT US

Gluten Free Kids was created for parents of children who have a gluten free diet

Those of you who follow a gluten free diet know how tricky when your kids come home from school
or a party asking for the same tasty treats all their freinds have had.

As parents we all know how hard it is to plan dinner every night without having dietary requirements
thrown into the mix.

We created this site for parents to share gluten free recipes that they and their kids enjoy with other families
looking for new things to try.

If you have other adults/children in your household who enjoy your gluten free treats then even better!

What is Gluten Free?

There is lots of information online about what it means to be gluten free.

Here is the wikipedia definition and also a a link to Coeliac UK
for you to learn more about it.

Who are we?
We are 3 mums who met on the morning school run. Our names are Sarah, Vanessa and Rosie

We all realised when attending the kids birthday parties that our children all had different
dietary requirements and started sharing recipes with each other.

We started a whatsapp group and during one of our regular chats we cam up with the crazy idea of
starting our own facebook page.

We were suprised at just how many other parents had children with food allergies & intolerances.

Lots of people had recipes to share so we decided to create a page that everyone could join and share
to save us having to do all the admin!

We hope everyone enjoys our site
Sarah, Vanessa and Rosie
